Government Support for Farmers

1. PM-Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)

Direct income support for small and marginal farmers.

  • ₹6,000 per year in 3 equal installments.
  • Funds transferred directly to the farmer’s bank account.
  • Over 12 crore farmers have benefited.
Learn more

2. Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Insurance coverage for crop loss due to natural calamities.

  • 2% premium for Kharif, 1.5% for Rabi crops.
  • Covers drought, floods, pests, etc.
  • Claim amount directly credited to farmer's account.
Learn more

3. Soil Health Card Scheme

Promotes balanced use of fertilizers based on soil testing.

  • Soil tested every 2 years.
  • Personalized nutrient report for optimal crop planning.
  • Reduces input cost and increases yield.
Learn more

4. Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

Credit facility for crop production and allied activities.

  • Loans up to ₹3 lakhs at interest as low as 4%.
  • For agriculture, animal husbandry, and fisheries.
  • Flexible repayment options.
Learn more

5. e-NAM (National Agriculture Market)

Online trading platform for farmers across India.

  • Integrated with 1,000+ mandis.
  • Real-time price discovery and transparency.
  • Eliminates middlemen, improves profits.
Learn more

6. Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana (PMKSY)

Improve water-use efficiency in agriculture.

  • Subsidy for drip and sprinkler irrigation systems.
  • “Har Khet Ko Pani” – water to every field.
  • Focus on micro-irrigation and watershed development.
Learn more

7. Sub-Mission on Agricultural Mechanization (SMAM)

Support for modern farming tools and machinery.

  • Up to 80% subsidy on farm equipment.
  • Focus on small and marginal farmers.
  • Support for custom hiring centers.
Learn more

8. Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY)

Support for agricultural innovation and infrastructure.

  • Funding for agri-startups and value chain development.
  • Focus on modern farming methods and training.
  • Flexible support for state-level priorities.
Learn more

9. National Mission on Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA)

Promote climate-resilient and sustainable farming practices.

  • Supports organic farming and rainwater harvesting.
  • Improves soil health and water-use efficiency.
  • Promotes agroforestry and climate-smart farming.
Learn more

10. Agri Infrastructure Fund

Loan support for building rural post-harvest infrastructure.

  • Interest subsidy on loans for warehouses and cold storage.
  • Open to farmers, FPOs, cooperatives, and agri-entrepreneurs.
  • Improves storage and market linkage opportunities.
Learn more

11. Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH)

Promote holistic development of fruits, vegetables, spices, and flowers.

  • Financial assistance for horticulture production and post-harvest management.
  • Support for nurseries, seed production, and organic farming.
  • Capacity building and market development initiatives.
Learn more

12. Market Intervention Scheme & Price Support Scheme (MIS-PSS)

Protects farmers from distress sales and price crashes.

  • Government procurement of perishable and price-sensitive crops.
  • Assures Minimum Support Price (MSP) for eligible crops.
  • Improves market stability and farmer income security.
Learn more
